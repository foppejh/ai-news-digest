# AI News Digest — 2026-06-09

## Executive Summary
Apple made a major AI architecture announcement, revealing a new framework built around Google Gemini models and launching a Core AI developer framework — a significant strategic pivot that reframes its previously cautious AI posture. OpenAI filed a confidential S-1 with the SEC, signaling a formal IPO process is underway. Xiaomi's MiMo team claimed a striking inference milestone: 1,000+ tokens/second on a 1-trillion-parameter MoE model using a standard 8-GPU server.
