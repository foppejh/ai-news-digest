# AI News Digest — 2026-06-25

## Executive Summary
Today's biggest AI stories center on a major IP dispute (Anthropic accusing Alibaba of illicitly extracting Claude's capabilities), two significant hardware milestones (OpenAI/Broadcom's custom "Jalapeño" inference chip and Cerebras's rocky post-IPO earnings), and a notable model release (GLM-5.2 for open agentic systems). Google also launched computer-use capabilities in Gemini 2.5 Flash, signaling an acceleration in agentic AI across the industry. Meanwhile, Google continues to face a brain drain of top AI researchers to competitors like Anthropic.

- [GLM-5.2 is a step change for open agents](https://www.interconnects.ai/p/glm-52-is-the-step-change-for-open) — *Interconnects / Hacker News* — GLM-5.2 is positioned as a meaningful leap for open-weight agentic AI, offering capabilities that could challenge proprietary models in autonomous task completion.
